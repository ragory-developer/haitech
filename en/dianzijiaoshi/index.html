E-classroom

Haitech e-classroom solution is a combination of advanced digitalization and networking. Through broadcasting teaching, group teaching, student demonstration and other functions, the interaction of multimedia classroom is greatly expanded. Through real-time test and assessment, it can effectively promote formative evaluation in the teaching process. Through screen locking, remote command, remote monitoring and other functions, the orderly progress of multimedia network classroom teaching is ensured. It can improve classroom efficiency and interest, reduce teachers' burden and broaden students' knowledge.
